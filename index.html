Projects

Mau Complex Forest Watch (MCFW)

Mau Complex Forest Watch (MCFW) is an initiative focused on promoting reforestation efforts across the Mau Forest Complex. This tool leverages Geographic Information System (GIS) and remote sensing to assess the potential areas for Tree Reforestation initiatives.


Status:

Ongoing

AquaSense Africa (ASA)

AquaSense Africa is a cutting-edge water quality monitoring platform that leverages advanced remote sensing and deep learning technologies to analyze and visualize key water quality parameters, including turbidity, chlorophyll concentration, and algal blooms across any African water body.


Status:

Ongoing

SpaceBorne AI Algorithm Rideshare Project

As part of the program by STAR.VISION Aerospace Group Limited & The Kenya Space Agency (KSA), this project focuses on the development of AI-Powered algorithm to help automate tea crop health monitoring from space.


Status:

Ongoing

EUDR Coffee Farmers' Compliance Monitoring Application (CFCMA)

A full stack Web GIS Application to determine coffee farmers' compliance with the European Union Deforestation Regulations (EUDR).


Status:

Completed, December 2024

FloodGuard Nairobi (FGN)

FloodGuard Nairobi (FGN) is a tool developed for flood disaster preparedness, monitoring, response, and risk reduction. Incorporated with various geospatial layers, the tool identifies flood-prone areas along riparian reserves, as well as flood vulnerability scores within Nairobi County.


Status:

Completed, November 2024
